Hi, I'm Adriana. Catlas is a playful and educational space for cat owners, cat enthusiasts, and anyone who would like to learn more about cats. Cats are amusing and mysterious creatures, and my personal experience with my own cat has inspired me to bring that joy and playfulness into my first web project. Catlas is my way of bringing the fascinating world of cats and their history to life, where learning feels fun and engaging.
